About Us

Welcome to Storecode, your go-to platform for seamless shopping experiences on social media.

At Storecode, we understand the challenges of sharing non-clickable links on social media platforms. That's why we've developed a unique code system to simplify the process for both influencers and buyers.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between influencers (Guides) and shoppers by providing a convenient and efficient way to share and discover products. With Storecode, shopping becomes a breeze, even on platforms where clickable links are not supported.

Thank you for choosing Storecode. Happy shopping!
